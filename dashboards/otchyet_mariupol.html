Mariupol City Administration: Citizen Appeals Reports (Jan 2023 – Jun 2024)

DOC-G2 · Social Conditions · Primary Sources · TOT Insights

Source note: These reports were produced by the occupation city administration of Mariupol and are primary sources from within the occupation apparatus. They reflect how the occupation administration chose to categorise and represent civilian demand. The most analytically significant feature is the reclassification of housing claims across the series — the drop from 66% to 7% between the first and second reports does not reflect resolved demand but a deliberate shift in reporting taxonomy. Read critically and against independent sources.
