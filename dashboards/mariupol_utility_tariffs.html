Utility tariff increases in occupied Mariupol, 2023–2025

Cumulative percentage increase per service, 2023 baseline to July 2025 tariff schedule

Sewerage costs rose 71% in two years. Waste collection increased by up to 68%. These increases compound on a population with collapsed independent income, creating structured financial dependency on the occupation administration.
