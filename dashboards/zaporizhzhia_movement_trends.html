Social Conditions  ·  Zaporizhzhia Oblast

Departure and return from occupied Zaporizhzhia Oblast, 2022 – spring 2025

Shares of total recorded population displacement flows, by destination

The data captures displacement across three phases: mass evacuation in 2022–23 as the occupation consolidated; onward migration to the EU, primarily through Russia or Belarus; departure to Russia (including forced or semi-coerced relocation); and a small return movement in spring 2025. Return flows remain minimal at 5% of total movement, consistent with entrapment dynamics documented across occupied territories.
